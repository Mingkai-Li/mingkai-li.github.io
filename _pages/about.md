Hi! I'm a senior undergraduate student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) working with [Xianghang Mi](https://xianghang.me/).

My research area is computer security, and I'm broadly interested in contributing to both the problem space and the solution space of computer security and privacy research. In the problem space, I'm curious in finding unknown security vulnerabilities and privacy breaches in the real world to call people's attention to the dark side of those day-to-day services. In the solution space, I'm especially interested in creating, enhancing, and utilizing technologies that can provide strong and provable security guarantees, e.g. decentralized systems, formal methods, trusted execution environments, etc.
